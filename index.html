About City Conference

The beautiful city of Portland, Oregon will be the host for City Conference !

Explore the beauty of Portland City with a passionate professionals. Discover new places and hidden secrets waiting for you to discover!

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international Portland community and spend entire day with some of the top experts, hobbyists, and enthusiasts? Be the first to know what can you get from City Conference

City Conference is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of listening and sharing.

What You'll Learn

History of Portland City Geography Economy Culture Sports Recreation

Speakers

Angie McHistory

Angie is a famous historian and teacher who is passionate about history, society, especially ones living in big cities like Portland!

GeoStradamus

GeoStradamus is a geographer who visited the whole world and now shares his knowledge to the new generations. He is a wise man!

Geo Economy

Geo loves money and everything associated with it. Especially he likes to talk about the economy of the city like Portland.

Ecma Culture

Ecma could talk for hours about the cultural offer of the city. She chose, however, the best treasures and will talk about them during a brief lecture.

Jay ProSport

Jay is a professional athlete. When only has free time comes to us and tells about his adventures during the competition. He can encourage the sport!

Json Recreation

All of his professional life, Json has worked with events organizers and devoted himself to planning the best possible ways for spending free time.
